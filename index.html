Forrest Gump

A story of how anything is possible

Here's a timeline of Forrest Gump's Life:

  • June 6th, 1944 - Forrest was born in the small town of Greebow, Alabama
    • August 11th, 1951 - 7 year old Forrest Gump receives leg braces due to a "crooked spine."
  • September 5th, 1951 - Forrest meets his soon-to-be bestfriend, Jenny Curran on the school bus
  • October 1st, 1951 - Forrest is able to break out of his leg brace and escape from his bullies
  • September 30th, 1961 - 17 year old Forrest outruns his bullies and ends up on a football field, where college football scouts see how fast he is
  • October 1st, 1962 - Forrest plays football at the University of Alabama. He was the best kicker in the nation
  • June 11th, 1963 - Alabama Governor George Wallace stood by the door of a auditorium to try to block the entry of two African American students, Vivian Malone and James Hood, to prevent them from registering at the university. Vivian dropped her books, which Forrest helped her pick right up.
  • 1963 - Forrest meets President John F. Kennedy
  • June 3rd, 1967 - Forrest travels to Vietnam with one of his best-friends he met in boot-camp, Bubba. This is where they meet Lieutenant Dan
  • June 7th, 1967 - Forrest Gump, Lt. Dan and Bubba are all ambushed in Vietnam. Bubba dies, Forrest gets shot in his buttocks, and Lt. Dan loses his legs
  • June 16th, 1967 - Forrest masters the game of ping-pong
  • November 19th 1968 - Forrest receives the Congressional Medal of Honor and gives a speech about war at a peace rally and then reunites with Jenny
  • July 20th, 1969 - Forrest teaches veterans how to play ping-pong as Neil Armstrong lands on the moon
  • January 1st, 1972 - Forrest becomes a national ping-pong champion and goes on live T.V. with John Lennon
  • June 15th, 1972 - President Richard Nixon awards Forrest for his amazing ping-pong abilities, and gives him a room in the Watergate Hotel
  • June 17th, 1972 - Forrest calls security guard Frank Wills to report a "power outage" in an office across his room. This call ultimately lead to the Watergate scandal.
  • August 8th, 1974 - Nixon reigns on TV, while Forrest returns home to Greenbow, Alabama receiving a check for $25,000 for doing a ping-pong advertisement
  • August 12th, 1974 - Forrest buys a shrimping boat with the money he received from the advertisement and names it S.S. Jenny.
  • August 29th, 1974 - Forrest and Lt. Dan steer through Hurricane Carmen
  • September, 1974 - Forrest and Lt. Dan catch thousands of shrimp while every other boat is destroyed. They have a monopoly on the local shrimp market and make a fortune
  • April 15th, 1975 - Ms. Gump passed away from cancer. Forrest retires from shrimping, leaving the proceeds to Lt. Dan
  • April 12th, 1976 - Lt. Dan invests his and Forrest's money in Apple Computers, and the pair became extremely wealthy
  • May, 1976 - Forrest funds the construction of a church and medical center
  • July 4th, 1976 - Forrest asks Jenny to marry her. She refuses, but they make love
  • July 5th, 1976 - Jenny leaves Forrest again. He goes out for a "little run". He ends up running across the nation four times
  • September 19th, 1979 - Forrest stops jogging around the country
  • March 30th, 1981 - Forrest receives a letter from Jenny. She insinuates he should come see her
  • April 1st, 1981 - Forrest meets Jenny and her son, Forrest Jr. She tells him that he is the father of her child
  • April 2nd, 1981 - Jenny tells Forrest about her unknown disease. She asks Forrest to marry her
  • April 26th, 1981 - Forrest and Jenny marry
  • March 22nd, 1982 - Jenny passes away from her disease
  • August 20th, 1982 - Forrest visits Jenny's grave and says goodbye
  • September 5th, 1982 - Forrest Jr. gets on his bus for his first day of school, just like Forrest Gump did 31 years prior
Forrest Gump was a "simple-minded" man who loved others. Not every can meet the President, play college Football, serve in a war, get a Medal of Honor, become a world-class athlete, start a national political scandal, become a business tycoon, survive the eye of a hurricane, make astonishing returns in the stock market, become a respected philanthropist, run across the country and marry their best friend. But Forrest Gump can. He teaches you that with perserverence, hard work and a little bit of luck, anything is possible.
"Life is like a box of chocolates, you never know what you're gonna get" - Forrest Gump
